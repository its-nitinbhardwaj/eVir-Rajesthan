Ticket Reservation

1. After successful logging in go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Select the "Book Ticket" option i.e. second in the list.

3. Fill the form at the left side of the portal and click the "GO" button.

4. Select the seat preference in the desired train.

5. Write the Aadhaar number in the box provided and select the seat preference.

6. Enter the OTP send to the mobile numbers linked with the Aadhaar Number.

7. After verification from the UIDAI you will be forwarded.

To the payment gateway.

* Do the transaction and you will be forwarded to the final ticket.

Tickets

1. Go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Select the "Tickets" option , the 2nd option from the top.

3. Click the desired option amongst the three.

4. Select a particular transaction to get the more details.

Boarding Confirmation

1. Go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Select "Boarding Stat." option , the 3rd option from the top.

3. Select your PNR Number from the "Drop Down Menu".

4. Press "GO" button.

5. Details of all the passengers will come in a list (subject to the terms and conditions).

6. Select the checkbox the passengers who are boarding on the train.

7. Select the checkbox "I agree the details provided above are true and real".

8. Click on the button "Confirm Boarding status".

9. A confirmation message will come on the screen and you are done.

Maintainance

1. Go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Select "Cleaning Req." option , the 4th option from the top.

3. Fill the required columns.

4. Click on Submit to post your request.

Food Booking

1. After successful logging in go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Choose the category on the basis of which you wish to book food.

2.1  Search by station-

  -Enter the station number or the name of the station you want your meal at.

  -Book the meal above than the lowest transaction limit and checkout.

  -Validate your PNR and confirm your order.

  -You will get the food on your seat on the booked station.

  -The payment will be cash on delivery.

3. Search by PNR-

  -Enter your PNR number.

  -Choose the station from the drop down list.

  -Select the food you want.

  -Check out.

4. Search by train number and date-

  -Enter the train number and date of journey.

  -Select the station you wnat food at.

  -Validate PNR.

  -Check out.

Emergency

1. Go to the Main Menu, (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal.

2. Select "Emergency" option , the 6th option from the top.

3. Select the PNR number, the emergency case and write the details (if yoy wish) and press "Emergency" button.

4. You will be provided with the Emergency-help as soon as possible in running train (if possible otherwise on the next coming station).

Train Tracking

1. Go to the Main Menu (Side Bar Button) at the left of your portal..

2. Select "Track" option , the 7th option from the top..

3. You will be provided with the details of the running train under your id..
